Privacy Policy

Bezique Online

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Overview

Bezique Online respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and how you can contact us with questions.

Bezique Online is a classic two-player card game. The app does not include gambling, betting, real-money prizes, casino mechanics, or unrestricted web access.

Information We May Collect

Depending on how the app is used and configured, Bezique Online may process the following types of information:

  • Player names or identifiers used to create, join, and manage matches
  • Gameplay and match data required to operate online games
  • Account or sign-in information, if login is required
  • Technical information such as device type, app version, diagnostics, crash reports, and performance data
  • Support messages, email addresses, and any information you choose to provide when contacting support

How We Use Information

Information is used only for purposes related to operating and improving Bezique Online, and providing advertising, including:

  • Creating, joining, and running online matches
  • Displaying player names or identifiers during gameplay
  • Saving and synchronizing match state
  • Maintaining app security and stability
  • Diagnosing crashes, bugs, and performance issues
  • Responding to support requests

Third-Party Services

Bezique Online may use third-party services such as Firebase to provide online gameplay, authentication, database functionality, security, diagnostics, crash reporting, and app stability. In addition, AdMob or other online advertising services may be used to provide anonymous marketing and advertising.

These services may process information according to their own privacy and security practices.

Data Retention

Gameplay and technical data may be retained for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain online matches, improve stability, resolve bugs, comply with legal obligations, and protect the app from misuse.

Support emails may be retained as long as needed to respond to your request and keep a record of support communication.

Your Choices

You may contact us to ask questions about this Privacy Policy or to request deletion of information associated with your use of the app, where applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
